What We Do

Solve The Problems Costing
Food Manufacturers Money Every Day

📉
Catch Orders That Would Otherwise Be Lost Through Understocks
When you run out of a product, you lose the sale — and sometimes the customer. Your historical data can predict this, but only if something is actually using it.
🗑️
Reduce Food Waste From Overstocks
Over-ordering perishables hits your margin twice: the cost of the stock itself, and the disposal cost when it expires. Accurate forecasts eliminate this guesswork.
🤖
ML Demand Forecasting
We build machine learning models trained on your own sales data to predict demand by product and week — so you order exactly what you need, when you need it.
⏱️
Reclaim Hours Lost to Manual Processes
Invoicing, supplier ordering, chasing new clients — these tasks are essential but repetitive. Every hour spent on admin is an hour not spent growing your production.
⚡
Build AI Agents That Handle the Admin
A custom AI agent can process invoices, draft supplier orders, or reach out to potential clients — running autonomously so your team focuses on what humans do best.
📊
Unlock Your Unused Data
Most small manufacturers are sitting on years of sales, production, and order data that nobody is reading. We turn that data into decisions your business can act on today.
